Who we are

Our company was founded by entrepreneurs with a proven track record of scaling companies and solving business issues on a global level. What began as a simple web and app offering to provide menus and restaurant information, evolved into a full-scale customer relationship management solution. We sought to understand the needs of restaurants and provide partners with the tools to reach and maintain customers. AppSuite has a historic partnership with POS integration software, Oracle Food and Beverage (formally known as Micros). We continue to create best-in-class technology and evolve offerings to power restaurant growth..
